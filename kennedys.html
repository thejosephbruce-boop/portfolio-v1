The Kennedys

The Kennedys is a 5-month creative course open to anyone trying to get into the creative industry. The selection process is geared up to provide a doorway for people who wouldn't normally see creative advertising as an option — and we get an incredible mix of brilliant young people from many different backgrounds.

Welcome to Kennadise Island

Do you know someone who is looking to jam their foot in the door of the advertising industry? Someone who is creatively minded, but has no previous agency experience, or ad-school training? Then send them our way.

We are recruiting for our 5-month crash-course in creative advertising at Wieden + Kennedy London. We have space for 8 incredibly talented creative, non-advertising newbies to get paid London Living Wage whilst getting trained to make incredible creative advertising.

You won't have to pay for this wisdom. We'll pay you. You won't need to worry about AI here. AI will worry about you. You won't need your own boat, laptop, trust-fund or nepo-daddy for this trip. We've got you covered.

Literally the opposite of uni.

2024 was my second year as Course Director and I'm incredibly excited for what the future of The Kennedys will hold. Find out more at thekennedyslondon.com.
