Projects

Joe Bruce, is an established Creative with over 16 years in the advertising industry. Below is an overview written in third person to make him sound important.

Beyond

Joe joined Beyond as Executive Creative Director in summer 2024 and has since made Creative Partner across the wider holding company (previously The Beyond Collective). A role that has seen him build a new creative culture within his department and across the wider business too.

In May 2025, Joe helped with the restructuring, redesigning and relaunching of Beyond, taking it from four separate companies to one unified agency brand and rebuilding, bolstering the creative department in the process.

In his short time at the agency, Joe brought in new clients like Find My Past, Mizkan Group and Sprite and Tabasco, as well as overseeing global projects for Yamaha Music and Jam Shed wines and creating local campaigns for Back Market, ARUK and Itsu.

W+K London

Before joining Beyond, Joe was Creative Director on the Nike account at Wieden + Kennedy London. A position that saw him running big campaigns and establishing new creative platforms in both local and global markets.

Before his time on Nike, Joe and his creative partner Albert Pukies looked after accounts like F1, Sainsbury's and Camden Town Brewery.

Alongside his day-to-day responsibilities at W+K, Joe was Creative Director for the Hanbury St window. He also served as the Course Director for The Kennedys; a 5-month program dedicated to nurturing a steady stream of new, young creative people into the ad industry.

About

Prior to his time at W+K, Joe was Creative Director at agencies 101 and Crispin Porter and Bogusky London, where he worked on a wide variety of brands, like Paddy Power, Tango, National Art Fund, Turkish Airlines, and Milka chocolate.

His first job in the industry was at BMB, where he worked on Ikea, Yorkshire Tea, Carling, Corona, Grolsch, William Hill, and Penguin Books.

Whilst at BMB, Joe helped launch the charity C.A.L.M in London, a cause that he remains very passionate about.

Entrepreneurship

Alongside his career in advertising, Joe is a founder and director of Bubble Bros Ltd. Starting out as an events bar at major festivals like Glastonbury, Lovebox, Wireless, and Download, it has since expanded into an on-trade sparkling wine wholesaler with products available in venues across the country (and more recently, the world).
