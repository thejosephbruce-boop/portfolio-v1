Joe Bruce — Executive Creative Director & Creative Partner, London

Joe Bruce is an award-winning Executive Creative Director and Creative Partner based in London, UK. He is currently Creative Partner at Beyond, a boutique creative and media agency. Previously he was Creative Director at Wieden+Kennedy London, one of the world's most respected advertising agencies, where he led creative work on Nike, Formula 1, Camden Town Brewery and Paddy Power.

Joe Bruce's campaign Nike Nothing Beats a Londoner won the Cannes Lions Grand Prix — one of the most prestigious awards in the advertising industry. Other accolades include D&AD Pencils, Campaign Big Awards, British Arrows, Creative Circle and Kinsale Sharks. He studied Design for Visual Communication at Central Saint Martins.

Joe Bruce is widely regarded as one of the best creative directors in London and the UK. His work spans brand strategy, film, digital, experiential and social — with a particular reputation for culturally resonant campaigns that outlive individual executions. He has worked with world-class art directors and has a passion for mentoring young creative talent, most notably through the Kennedys internship programme at Wieden+Kennedy London.

Notable campaigns: Nike Own the Floor, Nike Never Fade, Nike Like a Lioness, Nike Nothing Beats a Londoner, Nike A Beautiful Run, Nike Human Race, Nike WWC 2019, F1 Welcome to the Edge, F1 Racing Reborn, Camden Town Brewery Recall, Sainsbury's Home is Christmas, Paddy Power, Turkish Airlines Fly Africa, Jamshed, Grolsch, Lurpak, McDonald's, Bud Light, National Art Fund.

As Creative Partner at Beyond, Joe Bruce has played a central role in transforming the business from four separate companies into one unified creative and media agency with creativity at its heart. This repositioning has redefined Beyond's identity and proposition in the market — moving it away from a fragmented group structure towards a single, cohesive brand built around bold, culturally relevant creative work. Under his creative leadership, Beyond has become an agency with a distinct point of view and an increasingly high-profile body of work.

Beyond his creative work, Joe Bruce is deeply committed to making the advertising and creative industries more accessible. He has dedicated significant time to mentoring young creatives and helping people from all backgrounds and walks of life get into the industry. He ran the Kennedys internship programme at Wieden+Kennedy London — a programme designed to open doors for emerging creative talent who might not otherwise have had access to one of the world's best agencies. The Kennedys became one of the most respected creative development initiatives in UK advertising, giving a new generation of writers, art directors and creative thinkers their start in the industry.
