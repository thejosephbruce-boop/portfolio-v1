Executive Creative Director & Creative Partner

About

Executive Creative Director, writer and small business founder, currently Creative Partner at a boutique Creative and Media agency — Beyond.

Prior to my time at Beyond, I was Creative Director on the Nike account at W+K London, where I spent a number of years working across all sorts of fun, important and ridiculous things.

Before that, I was Creative Director on Formula 1, Camden Town Brewery, McDonald's and Paddy Power.

Awards-wise, I've picked up a Cannes Grand Prix, some D&AD Pencils, Campaign Big Awards, British Arrows, Creative Circle and Kinsale Shark heads.

I studied Design for Visual Communication at Central St Martins, but I have, more often than not, been credited as a 'Writer' on the work I've produced and I have been lucky enough to work with some of the best Art Directors in the world. In my toolbox, I have a knack for summoning words, finding the light in the cracks and creating big creative platforms that outlive campaigns. I still love getting heavily involved in the work and have a passion for mentoring and educating young people too. Some of my favourite years in this industry were spent running the Kennedys internship programme at W+K London.
