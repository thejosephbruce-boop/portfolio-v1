About

I know I know. Hear me out, I'll keep this short. I've spent 16+ years working at great advertising agencies, big and small. Most recently I've helped build a new creative department and improve the creative ambition at The Beyond Collective.

Prior to my time at Beyond, I was Creative Director on the Nike account at W+K London, where I spent a number of years working across all sorts of fun, important and ridiculous things.

I studied Design for Visual Communication at Central St Martins, but I have, more often than not, been credited as a writer on the work I've produced and have been lucky enough to work with some of the best Art Directors in the world. In my toolbox, I have a knack for summoning words, finding the light in the cracks and creating big creative platforms that outlive campaigns. I love getting the best out of my teams and have a passion for mentoring and educating young people too. Some of my favourite years in this industry were spent running the Kennedys internship programme at W+K London.
